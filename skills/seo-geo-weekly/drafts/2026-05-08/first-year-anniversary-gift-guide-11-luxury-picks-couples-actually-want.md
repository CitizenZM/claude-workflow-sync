<h3>Body-Safe Materials: The Non-Negotiable</h3>
<p>Luxury is synonymous with quality, and in the world of intimate goods, that means body-safe materials. Your skin is your largest organ, and anything that comes into close contact with it should be non-porous, phthalate-free, and easy to sanitize.</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Platinum-Cured Silicone:</strong> The gold standard for toys. It's non-porous, hypoallergenic, velvety to the touch, and easy to clean. It holds temperature well for sensation play.</li>
    <li><strong>Genuine & Vegan Leather:</strong> For restraints and impact toys, look for quality craftsmanship. Genuine leather is durable and softens over time, while high-quality vegan leather offers an ethical and equally beautiful alternative.</li>
    <li><strong>Polished Metals:</strong> Medical-grade stainless steel and aluminum are non-porous, durable, and excellent for temperature play.</li>
    <li><strong>What to Avoid:</strong> Be cautious with porous materials like jelly or TPR. They can harbor bacteria if not cared for meticulously and may contain phthalates, which can leach over time.</li>
</ul>
